Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our diet plans and services.

How long do the diet plans last?

Our initial plans are typically designed for 3 months, which allows sufficient time to establish new habits and see measurable results. After this period, you can choose to continue with maintenance plans or schedule periodic check-ins.

Are the meal plans flexible for different cuisines?

Absolutely! Our nutrition plans are customized to incorporate your cultural preferences, favorite cuisines, and regional ingredients. We can design plans that include traditional Indian dishes, international cuisines, or a blend of both.

How often will I meet with my nutritionist?

The consultation frequency depends on your specific plan, but typically includes an initial comprehensive consultation, followed by bi-weekly sessions for the first month, and then monthly follow-ups. Additional consultations can be scheduled as needed.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we design plans that accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, lactose-free, and other dietary preferences or restrictions. Our goal is to create a nutrition plan that works for your specific needs and lifestyle.
