The NutriFit Story

Founded in 2020, NutriFit emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to revolutionize the way people approach nutrition and wellness in their daily lives.

Our founder, Dr. Keshika, recognized that despite an abundance of nutritional information available, many individuals struggled to find personalized guidance that addressed their unique health concerns. Having witnessed firsthand the transformative power of tailored nutrition plans during her clinical practice, she set out to create a platform that would make personalized nutritional expertise accessible to everyone.

Today, NutriFit stands as a testament to that vision, with a team of certified nutritionists, dietitians, and wellness coaches dedicated to helping our clients achieve their health goals through science-backed, personalized nutrition plans.
